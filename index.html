About Marco & MARCOLLECTIVE

Marco Baciu is a Romanian jazz guitarist aiming to introduce audiences to a fresh sound rooted in the deep musical traditions of Transylvania. By combining rich jazz harmonies with steady hip-hop grooves and the distinctive melodic lines of Romanian folk, Marco is shaping a new wave of music that offers listeners something both innovative and deeply relatable.

MARCOLLECTIVE is a Maastricht (NL)based ensemble led by Marco Baciu that blends the improvisational spirit of jazz with the deep, steady grooves of hip-hop, all unified by the haunting melodic traditions of Transylvania. With its unique fusion of modern rhythm and ancestral sound, MARCOLLECTIVE creates a musical landscape that feels both innovative and deeply rooted, inviting listeners into a world where heritage and contemporary expression move as one.

The ensemble brings together four exceptional musicians: Giuseppe Gallitelli (Italy) on drums, blending jazz complexity with the steady groove of hip-hop; Valerio Ruperto (Italy) on bass, contributing versatility while keeping his clear musical identity; Dan Smit (Netherlands) on piano, whose energetic, genre-bending style drives the fusion of jazz, hip-hop, and Balkan rhythms; and Marco Baciu (Romania) on guitar, shaping the distinctive voice of the collective.

Marco's performances are characterized by a deep emotional resonance, where each note tells a story and every melody carries the weight of tradition while reaching toward contemporary expression. Through MARCOLLECTIVE, he creates music that moves peopleboth physically and emotionallychanneling energy, feel, and precision into every performance.
